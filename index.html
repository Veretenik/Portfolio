About Me

I am an animation student at Filmuniversität Babelsberg KONRAD WOLF with experience in 2D animation, stop-motion, 3D, games, and VR. I have worked as an animator and director on various student and professional projects.

My professional experience includes background and animation work at Shopski Animation Studio. I have worked in game development, contributing to visual development and animation for a 2D game at Flying Door Studio, as well as on a VR project.

I am the author and illustrator of the children's book "Anja und der Blumenzauber". I also regularly lead animation workshops for children, and I am actively involved in the organization of the Sehsüchte Film Festival, gaining valuable insight into festival programming and film culture from behind the scenes.

I am interested in collaborative, visually driven projects within animation studios, games, and interdisciplinary formats.
