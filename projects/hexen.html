Animation short film

Die Hexen
im Bremer Ratskeller

As director, I took part in the production of "Die Hexen im Bremer Ratskeller", a student project created by animation and music students at Filmuniversität Babelsberg Konrad Wolf. The film is made entirely in analog Lego brick animation. I designed the panoramic background and two characters, built the puppets, and animated seven scenes. I also edited and composited the film and handled production tasks.
